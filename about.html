About Skunk Squad Art, Code, and Community

Six years of drawing, coding, and crafting. Each Skunk is built from Procreate slides, refined by a custom Python rarity engine, and permanently stored on Arweave. This is the story of one artist's journey — where persistence meets blockchain.

The Story Behind the Squad

One artist. Six years. 10,000 unique skunks.

MontanaDad didn't set out to create just another NFT collection. This project started as an exploration of digital art, evolved through countless hours of sketching in Procreate, and transformed into a full-scale generative system powered by custom Python code.

Every trait was hand-drawn. Every slide was carefully crafted. Every combination was thoughtfully balanced through a proprietary rarity engine built from scratch. This isn't mass-produced art — it's a labor of love.

Six years of refinement. Thousands of iterations. One unwavering vision: to create something authentic in a space often dominated by hype over substance.

The Creative Process

From sketch to blockchain

✏️

Hand-Drawn in Procreate

Every single trait starts as a hand-drawn slide in Procreate. No templates. No shortcuts. Just an artist and a tablet, creating 150+ unique attributes across multiple layers.

🐍

Python Rarity Engine

A custom-built Python system analyzes trait combinations, ensures balanced rarity distribution, and generates 10,000 unique skunks. Each combination is mathematically verified for uniqueness.

🔒

Military-Grade Encryption

Every image and metadata file is individually encrypted before upload. Your NFT isn't just art — it's secured with the same encryption standards used by governments and financial institutions.

🌐

Permanent Arweave Storage

Stored forever on Arweave, not IPFS. No broken links. No disappeared images. Decentralized, permanent, and immutable — your art will outlive us all.

⛓️

ERC-721A Smart Contract

Deployed using the gas-optimized ERC-721A standard on Ethereum. Lower minting costs, proven security, and full NFT marketplace compatibility.
